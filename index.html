Why should one read books?

Well,there is no obvious answer because people read books for their own reasons.Some read as an hobby,some as an distraction from everyday life, or some read it to try to inculcate the habit.In my opinion one should read books because they help you increase your vocabulary,and help you get off computer,mobile and television screens.

PROS AND CONS

Pros- 1.You increase your vocabulary. 2.You inculcate a habit applauded by all. 3.You spend less time on computers and mobiles. Cons- Well, there are no cons!
